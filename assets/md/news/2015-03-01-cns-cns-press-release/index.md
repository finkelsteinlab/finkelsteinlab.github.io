# Two Assistant Profs Win Career Awards

![Ilya Finkelstein](/assets/md/news/2015-03-01-cns-cns-press-release/ilya-finkelstein.jpg)

Two College of Natural Sciences assistant professors have won the National Science Foundation's Faculty Early Career Development (CAREER) Award.

**Işıl Dillig** of the Department of Computer Science received a CAREER Award for her research into automatically testing the correctness of software.

**Ilya Finkelstein** of the Department of Molecular Biosciences received a CAREER Award to study mechanisms of genome maintenance using a unique combination of biochemistry, single-molecule microscopy and microfluidics.

The NSF CAREER Award is among the most prestigious awards for junior faculty and comes with five years of research funding.
