# HHMI Selects 45 International Student Research Fellows

Yoori Kim was selected as one of 45 recipients of the prestigious [HHMI International Student Research Fellowship](https://www.hhmi.org/news/hhmi-selects-45-international-student-research-fellows). The fellowship supports outstanding international students pursuing Ph.D.s at U.S. institutions in the biomedical sciences.
