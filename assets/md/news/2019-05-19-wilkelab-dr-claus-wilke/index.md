# Wilke Lab

The [Wilke Lab](https://wilkelab.org/) at The University of Texas at Austin, led by Dr. Claus O. Wilke, focuses on computational biology, molecular evolution, and bioinformatics.

Kuang Hu joined the Finkelstein Lab in 2019 as a joint graduate student with Dr. Claus Wilke.
