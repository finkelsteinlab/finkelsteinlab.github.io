# William H. Press

![William H. Press](/assets/md/news/2020-07-16-oden-dr-bill-press/bill-press.jpg)

**Professor**, Oden Institute for Computational Engineering and Sciences, The University of Texas at Austin

Also holds appointments in the Department of Computer Science and Department of Integrative Biology.

Dr. Press is a member of the National Academy of Sciences and a fellow of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences. His research spans computational biology, numerical methods, and information theory applied to biological systems.

Collaborations with the Finkelstein lab include work on DNA-based information encoding and indel-correcting DNA barcodes.
