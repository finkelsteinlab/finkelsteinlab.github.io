Welcome to the Finkelstein Lab blog! This is where I'll share thoughts on science, methods, tools, and occasional quick links.

## What to expect

I plan to write about:

- **Essays**: Longer reflections on science and research
- **Quick links**: Interesting papers, tools, and resources
- **Protocols**: Lab methods and computational workflows
- **Paper summaries**: Journal club-style notes on papers we're reading

## Why a blog?

I've been meaning to write more publicly for a while. A blog feels like the right format—somewhere between the formality of papers and the ephemerality of social media.

## Technical notes

This blog is built with Jekyll, the same static site generator that powers our main lab website. Posts are written in org-mode and converted to Markdown via Pandoc, with citations pulled from our Zotero library.

Stay tuned for more!
